TAXI FARE PREDICATION

Taxi fare prediction is an important application of machine learning in the transportation industry. The use of decision tree, random forest, and XGBoost algorithms can significantly improve the accuracy of taxi fare predictions. When applied to taxi fare prediction, these algorithms can take into account various factors that affect the fare, such as distance traveled, time of day, traffic conditions, and weather. By analyzing historical data, the algorithms can learn to make accurate predictions about the cost of a taxi ride in a particular location. Overall, the use of decision tree, random forest, and XGBoost algorithms can significantly improve the accuracy and efficiency of taxi fare prediction, benefiting both passengers and drivers.
